About Us

About The Whitehorse Funeral Services

When grief touches your life, the weight of decisions and arrangements can feel overwhelming. At The Whitehorse Funeral Services, we lift that burden with compassionate care and unwavering support. We're a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about providing personalized funeral services that honor the wishes of the deceased and comfort those left behind.

We understand that every family is unique, and we take the time to listen, understand your needs, and create a service that truly reflects the life and spirit of your loved one. From the smallest detail to the most significant arrangement, we're here to guide you every step of the way, ensuring that your experience is as seamless and stress-free as possible. Our commitment is to provide a safe and supportive space for you to grieve, heal, and celebrate the life of your loved one.

Our Mission

At Whitehorse Funeral Services, our mission is to provide compassionate, dignified, and personalized funeral services that honor the lives of loved ones and support grieving families through difficult times.

Compassion, Respect, Integrity, Professionalism, Personalization and community are our core values. Our vision is to be the leading provider of funeral services in Nairobi and Beyond, recognized for excellence in service, integrity, and community support.

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and respect, ensuring that every family we serve feels supported and cared for during their time of need.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do when a loved one passes away?

+

When a loved one passes away, first contact Whitehorse Funeral Services at our 24-hour hotline. We'll guide you through the immediate steps, including transportation of the deceased, death certification, and beginning the funeral planning process. Our compassionate team is available to assist you through every stage during this difficult time.

How do I plan a funeral service with Whitehorse?

+

Planning a funeral service with us begins with a consultation where we discuss your needs, preferences, and budget. We'll guide you through selecting services such as transportation, casket options, floral arrangements, and ceremony details. Our team will handle all logistics while you approve each element, ensuring the service honors your loved one appropriately.

How much does a funeral service cost?

+

Funeral costs vary based on the services selected. We offer packages starting from basic services to comprehensive arrangements. During our initial consultation, we provide a transparent breakdown of all costs and work with your budget to create a dignified service. We also offer guidance on available payment options and assistance programs if needed.

Can you accommodate cultural and religious customs?

+

Yes, we respectfully accommodate diverse cultural and religious customs. Our experienced staff has served families from various backgrounds and traditions. We work closely with religious leaders and community members to ensure all ceremonies honor your specific customs, from traditional Catholic services to Hindu cremation rituals and everything in between.

Do you offer pre-planning funeral services?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive pre-planning services that allow you to make arrangements in advance. This relieves your loved ones of difficult decisions during their time of grief and ensures your wishes are honored. Our pre-planning consultants can guide you through options, document your preferences, and provide information about pre-payment plans if desired.

What support do you offer families after the funeral?

+

Our care extends beyond the funeral service. We provide grief support resources, including counseling referrals, support group information, and memorial planning assistance. We also help with practical matters such as obtaining death certificates, assisting with obituary publications, and coordinating with insurance companies regarding funeral benefits.

Can you help with transportation arrangements?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive transportation services. Our professional fleet includes hearses and family limousines for the funeral procession. We also handle the dignified transport of the deceased from the place of death to our funeral home. For international arrangements, we can coordinate with airlines and manage all necessary documentation for repatriation services.
